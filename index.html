Our Story

Built With Our Hands

We invite you to see the raw, real journey of how MQLC came to be. From the first brick to the final touches, our community poured its sweat and resources into this center. We laid bare the process so you can see exactly where every contribution goes.

Now, as we look to our future scope, we need your help to write the next chapters.

Before We Began

An empty space brimming with potential. This is where the dream of MQLC took its very first breath.

Material

Procuring the highest quality materials to ensure our foundation stands the test of time.

Roof & Water Proofing

Securing the sanctuary from the elements, ensuring a safe and dry environment for learning.

Plumbing & Fabrication

Running the lifeblood through walls and setting the steel framework for safety and structure.

Electrical Work

Wiring the center for light and learning. Safety and illumination across all classrooms.

Tiling

Setting individual tiles with precision to ensure a clean, durable surface to walk and pray on.

Painting

Coating the bare walls to bring life, warmth, and our signature colors to the interior.

Carpenter Work

Crafting custom wooden fixtures, doors, and storage that breathe elegance into the space.
